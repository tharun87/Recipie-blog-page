Masala Maggie


Ingredients

  • 140 gm maggi noodles
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 12 gm maggi masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 1/4 tablespoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala powder
  • salt as required
  • 2 1/2 cup water

Process


  1. Boil water & chop the veggies:

    To prepare this mouth-watering snack recipe, put a deep-bottomed pan over medium flame and add water in it and bring it to a boil. Meanwhile, take a chopping board and finely chop onion, capsicum, tomato and green chilli on it. Once the water begins to boil, add Maggi masala noodles and peas to it. Let it cook till the noodles and peas are soft and tender.

  2. Cook Maggi and saute veggies in a separate pan:

    Next, in another pan, add butter and let it melt. Then, add chopped onion and saute till it changes colour. Now, add garlic paste, chopped capsicum, tomato and green chilli to it and saute well for a couple of minutes, till the tomatoes are soft.

  3. Add spices:

    Next, in the sauteed vegetables, add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, black pepper powder and saute for a minute. Now, add the boiled Maggi noodles and peas to this masala and mix well and cook for 1 more minute.

  4. Garnish and enjoy:

    Once done, transfer to a serving bowl and sprinkle a pinch of chaat masala over the noodles to make it even more flavorful. Serve it hot to enjoy!


Vegetable Maggi Cutlets Recipe


Ingredients

  • 150 gm maggi noodles
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 5 tablespoon corn flour
  • 1/2 cup grated cabbage
  • 1 tablespoon maggi masala
  • 1 1/2 cup refined oil
  • salt as required
  • 2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 2 cup water

  1. Step 1

    To make this snack recipe, place a pan over medium flame and add water in it. Once the water starts boiling, add Maggi noodles along with Maggi masala in it. Combine well and cook for 6-8 minutes. Once done, turn off the flame and transfer the cooked Maggi to a bowl. Keep it aside to cool

  2. Step 2

    Once done, add grated cabbage and carrots, corn flour, salt and chilli flakes to the cooked maggi. Using your hands, mix all the ingredients well and divide into equal portions. Make round patties with palm.

  3. Step 3

    Next, place a non-stick pan over medium flame and add oil to it. Once the oil is hot enough, place the patties one by one in the pan in batches and deep fry them on low flame until golden brown from both sides. Once fried, place these cutlets on a kitchen towel to absorb the extra oil.

  4. Step 4

    The Vegetable Maggi Cutlets are now ready, serve hot with tomato ketchup or mint chutney to enjoy!
